TSincFilter

Properties Methods Events

Unit: ACS_Filters

Descends from: TACSConverter

TSincFilter component implements a variety of filters based on a sinc kernel. This component takes in an audio stream, performs one of the filtering operations on it and passes the resulting stream to the next component in the audio-processing chain. Note that this component operates on 16-bit streams only.
