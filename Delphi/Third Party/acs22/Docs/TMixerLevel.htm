TMixerLevel

Unit: ACS_Mixer

TMixerLevel = record
 case Word of
 1 :
(
Left, Right : Byte;
);
2 : (Main : Byte;);
end;

This record is used to read/set level values for stereo/mono mixer channels. Left is the left channel and Right is the right channel for a stereo mixer channel. Main is used for mono mixer channels.
